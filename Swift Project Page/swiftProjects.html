Task Quest

Still in development

Task Quest is a role-playing game style reminder's app. I felt typical reminder apps are very dull and don't provide an incentive to get things done. Task Quest fixes that problem by giving the Hero, You!, experience points once you complete a quest. By completing each quest those experience points go towards your hero where you can upgrade their skill set and even give them armor pieces to get bonuses!

Task Quest shows all your active quests right away so you can see how much or how little you have left. The more you do the better your hero becomes!


From the main screen you also have access to

  • Your recently completed Quests.
  • Add a new quest.
  • View your hero.
  • Various settings options.

Creating a new Quest in Task Quest is as simple as tapping the + button in the upper corner.

The quest input screen will pop up prompting you to enter your next quest. Sticking with traditional RPG elements you can add

  • Your quest title. What needs to be done?
  • Who assigned this quest to you?
  • There is a possibility that there is more to the quest than just the title! Add some extra notes to aid in its completion.
  • Enter when the quest needs to be completed by.
  • Things change! So any part of your quest can be changed.

Here is your newly inputted quest. It shows everything that you just inputted with a remaining time to complete your quest.

Here you can also edit any part of the quest if something happens to change.

Once you have completed your quest tap the Complete Quest button to get your experience! Unless some other hero had the same task and finished it before you, it can be removed from your active quests.
