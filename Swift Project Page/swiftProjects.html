Task Quest

Task Quest is a role-playing game (RPG) style reminder's app. I felt typical reminder apps are very dull and don't provide an incentive to get things done. Task Quest fixes that problem by giving the Hero, You!, experience points once you complete a Quest. By completing each quest those experience points go towards your hero where you can upgrade their skill set and even give them armor pieces to get bonuses!

    A hero needs Quests to do!
    Following RPG style quest giving, Task Quest asks for:
    1. A quest name
    2. Who gave the current quest
    3. Extra notes critical to the quest
    4. Finally, a deadline to be set to complete the task.

    Create as many quests as you need to do, the more the better.
    Check all your currently active Quests

    Choose how you want to view them. Sort by:
    1. Time until completion
    2. Alphabetical
    3. First added or last added

    Remove a quest at the table view
    Every quest can be edited. It's a living world, things can change!
    The quest might have changed slightly... Change the title!
    The quest might need to be completed by someone else... Change who gave it to you1
    You might have extra time to complete the quest... Change the completion time1
    There might be extra steps involved now... Add it to the notes!

    The Quest might not even need to be completed anymore, so it can also be deleted from here or from the table view.

    If the Quest has been completed, tap Complete Quest to get your well deserved XP!
    Once you have completed your Quests, they will appear here where you can see all of the hard work you have done.
